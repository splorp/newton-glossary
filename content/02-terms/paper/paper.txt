Title: Paper

----

Date: 23 November 2017

----

Text:

The document format (or [stationery](/terms/stationery)) used by the word processor component of the [Newton Works](/terms/newton-works) application. The Newton Works word processor does not utilize [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition), so text must be entered into the word processor using a keyboard.
