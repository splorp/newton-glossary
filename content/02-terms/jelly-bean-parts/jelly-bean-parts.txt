Title: Jelly Bean Parts

----

Date: 07 February 2017

----

Text:

An assortment of individual electronic components which can be replaced by a single multifunction chip or [chipset](/terms/chipset). Mike Culbert, a member of the [Newton Systems Group](/terms/newton-systems-group), used the term to explain why [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) chose to use the [Voyager](/terms/voyager) chipset in later Newton devices.
