Title: Project Newtonberg

----

Text:

A website instigated by Rob West in 1996 that collected resources related to open source and public domain texts available in [Newton Book](/terms/newton-book) format. Project Newtonberg takes its name from the original [Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/).
