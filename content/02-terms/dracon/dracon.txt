Title: Dracon

----

Text:

A division of [Harris Corporation](http://harris.com/) that designed and manufactured telephony test equipment. Dracon developed the [SuperTech 2000](/terms/supertech-2000), a ruggedized [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device used by telecommunication field technicians.
