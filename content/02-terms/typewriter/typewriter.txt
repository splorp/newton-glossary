Title: Typewriter

----

Date: 31 August 2017

----

Text:

The default onscreen [keyboard](/terms/keyboard) that appears when the [keyboard button](/terms/keyboard-button) is [tapped](/terms/tapped). The typewriter keyboard is designed for easily entering text into [applications](/terms/application) such as [Notepad](/terms/notepad). Several other onscreen keyboards are available for entering specific types of data, including [Numeric](/terms/numeric), [Phone](/terms/phone), and [Time/Date](/terms/time-date).
