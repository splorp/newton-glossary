Title: Tap

----

Text:

To touch the screen briefly with the [pen](/terms/pen). Essentially the same action as clicking a mouse or trackpad button on a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer), [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer), or equivalent device.
