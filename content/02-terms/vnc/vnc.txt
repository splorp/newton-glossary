Virtual Network Computing

A platform independent, open source mechanism for viewing and interacting with the screen of a remote computer system over a network. VNC was originally developed by the [Olivetti & Oracle Research Laboratory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olivetti_Research_Laboratory) in the late 1990s.
