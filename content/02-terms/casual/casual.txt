Title: Casual

----

Text:

One of the default bitmap [fonts](/terms/font) on the Newton. Casual is used when the [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) engine converts printed text into [recognized text](/terms/recognized-text).

_This entry requires a screenshot._
