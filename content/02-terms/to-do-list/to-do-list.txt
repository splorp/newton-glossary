Title: To Do List

----

Date: 09 November 2017

----

Text:

A daily [checklist](/terms/checklist) in the [Date Book](/terms/date-book) application which allows the user to create and prioritize tasks. Tasks are marked as completed by [tapping](/terms/tapping) a [checkbox](/terms/checkbox). Uncompleted tasks are carried over to the next day.
