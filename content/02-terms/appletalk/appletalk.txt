Title: AppleTalk

----

Text:

A routable networking [protocol](/terms/protocol) developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) for communications between Mac OS computers and other platforms over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network). AppleTalk is a variation of [RS-422](/terms/rs-422) serial communication and is supported by physical networking such as [LocalTalk](/terms/localtalk) (originally also called AppleTalk) and [Ethernet](/terms/ethernet). AppleTalk over Ethernet in known as [EtherTalk](/terms/ethertalk).
