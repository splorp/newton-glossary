Title: Book Maker Commands

----

Text:

A set of text-based [Newton Book Maker](/terms/newton-book-maker) instructions used to tag and differentiate specific content items, such as text or graphics, in a Newton [book source file](/terms/book-source-file). For example, specifying the title and layout of a [Newton book](/terms/newton-book) require different commands.

Sometimes referred to as [dot commands](/terms/dot-commands) because the commands must begin with a period or dot.
