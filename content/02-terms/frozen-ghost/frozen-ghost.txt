Title: Frozen Ghost

----

Text:

An alternate name for the clear case [Frozen Newton](/terms/frozen-newton) project, which involved the creation of a clear case [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100).

Definitely not a reference to the [Canadian rock band](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frozen_Ghost) from the 1980s.
