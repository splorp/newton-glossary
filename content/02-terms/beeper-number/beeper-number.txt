Title: Beeper Number

----

Date:

----

Text:

An alternate name for the seven-digit personal identification number or [PIN](/terms/pin) associated with an [Apple Wireless Messaging Service](/terms/apple-wireless-messaging-service) account. The term “beeper” is derived from the sound made by old school wireless paging devices when receiving a message.
