Title: Time/Date

----

Date: 31 August 2017

----

Text:

An onscreen [keyboard](/terms/keyboard) that appears when the [keyboard button](/terms/keyboard-button) is [tapped](/terms/tapped). The time/date keyboard is designed for easily entering times and dates into [applications](/terms/application) such as [Date Book](/terms/date-book). Several other onscreen keyboards are available for entering specific types of data, including [Typewriter](/terms/typewriter), [Numeric](/terms/numeric), and [Phone](/terms/phone).
