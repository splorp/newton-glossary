Title: Lindy

----

Text:

The code name of the [MessagePad 110](/terms/messagepad-110) device. Sometimes mistakenly referred to as [Lizzy](/terms/lizzy). An accompanying charging [cradle](/terms/cradle) for the Lindy device was known as the “[Lindy Crib](/terms/lindy-crib)”.
