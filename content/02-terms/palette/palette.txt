Title: Palette

----

Text:

A small [floating container view](/terms/floating-container-view) that provides [controls](/terms/control) for modifying the contents of other [views](/terms/view). The palette appears on top of other views of the same [application](/terms/application) and remains open for use in all visible views. The user can move the palette using a [drag handle](/terms/drag-handle).
