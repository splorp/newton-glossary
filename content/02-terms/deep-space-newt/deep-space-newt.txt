Title: Deep Space Newt

----

Date: 16 February 2017

----

Text:

A game for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) developed by [DejaVu Software](/sources/dejavu) derived from the original text-based [Star Trek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Trek_(text_game)) game. Also known as [DSNewt](/terms/dsnewt), DejaVu ported the game to the iOS platform in 2008.
