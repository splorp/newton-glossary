Title: Tapped.In

----

Date: 04 November 2017

----

Text:

A monthly email newsletter published by the [Newton Systems Group](/terms/newton-systems-group) between October 1994 and May 1997. The target audience of the newsletter focused on systems integrators, value added resellers, and independent software vendors who were representing the Newton platform.
