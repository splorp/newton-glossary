Title: Routing

----

Text:

The process of sending or receiving data through the [In/Out Box](/terms/in-out-box) application using a communications [transport](/terms/transport) such as printing, faxing, [beaming](/terms/beaming), or emailing. Routing is generally initiated by using one of the options found in the [action picker](/terms/action-picker).
