Title: Inkwell

----

Text:

The Mac OS version of the Newton [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system, eventually known simply as the [Ink](/terms/ink) preference pane in early releases of Mac OS X. The Inkwell interface consisted of the [InkBar](/terms/inkbar) toolbar and the [InkPad](/terms/inkpad) application, a floating window where the user can write and draw.
