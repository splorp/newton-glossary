Title: Dr Llama

----

Date:

----

Text:

The nom de plume of [Maurice Sharp](/sources/maurice-sharp), who worked as a developer technical support engineer in the [Newton Systems Group](/terms/newton-systems-group) at [Apple](https://www.apple.com/). The nickname stems from the unofficial mascot of Apple’s [Developer Technical Services](/terms/developer-technical-services) team … the [Llama](/terms/llama). For reasons yet to be determined, Sharp was also known as [Commander Clueless](/terms/commander-clueless) within the group.
