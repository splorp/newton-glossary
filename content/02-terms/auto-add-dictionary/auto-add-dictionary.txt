Title: Auto-Add Dictionary

----

Date: 14 October 2017

----

Text:

A list of words that have been automatically added to the [user dictionary](/terms/user-dictionary). If the auto-add dictionary is not empty, the [Recently Written Words](/terms/recently-written-words) slip displays its contents when the user opens the [Personal Word List](/terms/personal-word-list) slip.

The auto-add dictionary is limited to 100 words.
