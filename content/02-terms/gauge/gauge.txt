Title: Gauge

----

Text:

An [object](/terms/object) with a marker that indicates an amount, degree, or value in relation to a range of possible values. A user can only read a gauge, not manipulate it like a [slider](/terms/slider) or other [user interface](/terms/user-interface) element.
