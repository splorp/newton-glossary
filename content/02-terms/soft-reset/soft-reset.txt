Title: Soft Reset

----

Text:

A method of [resetting](/terms/reset) the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) by pressing the [reset button](/terms/reset-button). A soft reset does not erase any user data, [applications](/terms/application), packages, [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) training, or preferences.

Also known as a [warm boot](/terms/warm-boot) or [software reset](/terms/software-reset).

## Additional Information

[Apple Support: Newton MessagePad: Different Types of Reset](https://support.apple.com/kb/TA33869)
