Title: Grouper 100MPS+

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the [Grouper](/terms/grouper) family of devices developed by [Digital Ocean](/sources/digital-ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided [wireless AppleTalk](/terms/wireless-appletalk) connectivity over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network). Identical in functionality to the [Grouper 100MPS](/terms/grouper-100mps), with additional hardware support for external data collection and output, such as barcode scanners, magnetic card readers, printers, and other serial devices with a [Mini-DIN](/terms/mini-din) connector. Also referred to as a [100MPS+](/terms/100mps-plus).
