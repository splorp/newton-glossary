Title: Grouper 100LT

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the [Grouper](/terms/grouper) family of devices developed by [Digital Ocean](/sources/digital-ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided [wireless AppleTalk](/terms/wireless-appletalk) connectivity over a [local area network](/terms/local-area-network). Identical in functionality to the [Grouper 100MP](/terms/grouper-100mp) and [Grouper 110MP](/terms/grouper-110mp), but specifically designed for Mac OS computers.

Also referred to as a [100LT](/terms/100lt).
