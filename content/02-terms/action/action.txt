Title: Action

----

Text:

An event or behaviour that is triggered when a specific item is selected in an [action picker](/terms/action-picker) or a specific [request word](/terms/request-word) is processed by the [assist](/terms/assist) function.

For example, entering the “call” request word into the [assist drawer](/terms/assist-drawer) will trigger an action to dial a phone number.
