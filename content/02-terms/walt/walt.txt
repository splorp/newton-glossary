----

Date:

Text:

Wizzy Active Lifestyle Telephone

An experimental telecommunication device developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) and [BellSouth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BellSouth) around the same time as the [Original MessagePad](/terms/original-messagepad). It featured a [touch screen](/terms/touch-screen) display and [pen](/terms/pen) similar to a Newton device, but with a HyperCard-based [user interface](/terms/user-interface) running on top of the Macintosh System 6.0.8 [operating system](/terms/operating-system).

The WALT has been referred to as “… a Mac in Newton’s clothing.”
