Title: Folder

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A place where items such as [notes](/terms/note), [cards](/terms/card), and [packages](/terms/packages) can be filed. [Global folders](/terms/global-folder) appear in all [applications](/terms/application) and [local folders](/terms/local-folder) appear only in the application where they were created.
