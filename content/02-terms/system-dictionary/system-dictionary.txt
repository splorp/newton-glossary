Title: System Dictionary

----

Date: 27 October 2017

----

Text:

A default [dictionary](/terms/dictionary) supplied by [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) which defines commonly used words and [grammar](/terms/grammar) constructs used by the text recognizers. System dictionaries are either an [enumerated dictionary](/terms/enumerated-dictionary) or a [lexical dictionary](/terms/lexical-dictionary).
