Title: Hot Removal

----

Date: 25 October 2017

----

Text:

A feature pertaining to a device which allows the user to remove a [PC card](/terms/pc-card) without having to power down the device. The [Voyager](/terms/voyager) chipset used in the [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000), [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100), and [eMate 300](/terms/emate-300) devices includes a [PCMCIA](/terms/pcmcia) controller which supports hot insertion of PC cards.

The opposite of hot insertion is [hot insertion](/terms/hot-insertion).
