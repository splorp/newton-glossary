Title: Yak

----

Date: 05 December 2017

----

Text:

The first [text-to-speech](/terms/text-to-speech) engine for [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), developed by John M. Saccente in 1995. Yak was a precursor to the more advanced [MacInTalk](/terms/macintalk) speech synthesis software available for later Newton devices.
