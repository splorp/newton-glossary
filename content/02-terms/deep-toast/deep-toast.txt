Title: Deep Toast

----

Text:

The status or [charge state](/terms/charge-state) of a rechargeable [battery](/terms/battery) in a Newton device that can no longer effectively hold a charge. The status message appears as [deepToast](/terms/deeptoast) in the battery test section of the [NewtTest](/terms/newttest) utility.

Deep Toast can also refer to a catastrophic hardware failure, such as a fatal hard drive crash.
