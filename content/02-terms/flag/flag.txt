Title: Flag

----

Text:

A value that is set either on or off to enable a feature. Typically, flag values are single bits, although they can be comprised of groups of bits or an entire byte.

A flag is also a keyword added to a [Newton Book Maker](/terms/newton-book-maker) command to enable a feature.
