Title: GFUN

----

Text:

Groupe Francophone des Utilisateurs de Newton

A support community for French speaking Newton users operated by Dominique Benet. GFUN was superseded by the similarly named [NGFUN](/terms/NGFUN) (Nouveau Groupe des Utilisateurs Francophones de Newton).
