Title: Emporium

----

Text:

The permanent internal description of a place where a user interacts with their Newton device, commonly referred to as a [locale](/terms/locale) or [worksite](/terms/worksite). Examples of named locales would be “Home” and “Office”. Choosing an emporium sets up location-specific information such as the area code, dialing prefixes, time zone, and so forth.

The plural of emporium is emporia. Now you know.
