Title: Electroluminescent Panel

----

Date:

----

Text:

An illumination technology consisting of a thin film phosphor layer sandwiched between a substrate. When charged, the phosphor in that emits visible light. The electroluminescent [backlight](/terms/backlight) found in Newton devices starting with the [MessagePad 130](messagepad-130) has a distinctive bright green colour. Also referred to as an [ELP](/terms/elp).
