Title: Tibet

----

Text:

Software developed by Russell Tait of [Compass Information Systems](https://web.archive.org/web/20010721235601/http://compassinfo.com/) that allows users to capture, store, and share images using a Newton device and a digital camera. Tibet is compatible with any digital camera with a serial port and firmware implementing the [Sierra Imaging](https://web.archive.org/web/20000706203351/http://www.sierraimaging.com/) chipset.
