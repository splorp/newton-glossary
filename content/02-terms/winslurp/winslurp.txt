Title: WinSlurp

----

Date: 10 December 2017

----

Text:

A user-friendly frontend for the [Slurpee](/terms/slurpee) data transfer utility. Developed by Mark Heringer, WinSlurp replaces the command line with a more approachable Windows-based [user interface](/terms/user-interface).
