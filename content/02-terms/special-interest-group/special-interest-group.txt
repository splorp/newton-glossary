Title: Special Interest Group

----

Text:

A like-minded collection of individuals who concentrate their knowledge, skills, and abilities on a special theme, technology, or goal. A [Newton User Group](/terms/newton-user-group) could be considered to be a special interest group dedicated to Newton hardware, software, support, and community. Also referred to as simply [SIG](/terms/sig).
