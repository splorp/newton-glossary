Title: EZXfer

----

Text:

A software utility by [EZShow Systems](/sources/ezshow-systems) which facilitates the transfer of [Microsoft PowerPoint 97](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_PowerPoint) slides from a Windows computer to a [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) device. PowerPoint slides are converted to a [package file](/terms/package-file) which can be transferred to the Newton device for use with the [EZPresenter](/terms/ezpresenter) presentation software.
