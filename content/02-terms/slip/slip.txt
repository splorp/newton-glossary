Title: Slip

----

Text:

A [container view](/terms/container-view) designated with a [matte border](/terms/matte-border) that an [application](/terms/application) displays to obtain detailed user [input](/terms/input), or to present alternatives which a user may choose to determine the outcome of a task that has been started.

_This entry requires a screenshot._
