Title: Soupervisor Mechanism

----

Date:

----

Text:

The system service that presents the user with information about a [soup](/terms/soup) when the user [taps](/terms/tap) a [soup icon](/terms/soup-icon) in the [Extras Drawer](/terms/extras-drawer). This facilitates the filing or moving of soup [entries](/terms/entry).
