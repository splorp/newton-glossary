Title: CDPD

----

Text:

Cellular Digital Packet Data

A defunct wireless data service developed in the early 1990s. CDPD was available as a networking option in the ruggedized [Seahorse](/terms/seahorse) and [Tarpon](/terms/tarpon) devices manufactured by [Digital Ocean](https://web.archive.org/web/19970124001013/http://digitalocean.com/).
