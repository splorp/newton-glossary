Title: Button Bar

----

Date:

----

Text:

A thick black [line](/terms/line) with graphical [buttons](/terms/button) on it. The [separator bar](/terms/separator-bar) and [status bar](/terms/status-bar) found in most [applications](/terms/application) are both examples of buttons bars.

Button bar also refers to the set of [icons](/terms/icon) located at the bottom of the Newton [screen](/terms/screen) which are used to launch applications. On [MessagePad 2000](/terms/messagepad-2000) and [MessagePad 2100](/terms/messagepad-2100) devices, the button bar contains user modifiable [soft buttons](/terms/soft-button), all other Newton devices utilize a permanent set of [hard buttons](/terms/hard-button).
