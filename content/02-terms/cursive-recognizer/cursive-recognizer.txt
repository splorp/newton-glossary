Title: Cursive Recognizer

----

Date:

----

Text:

The algorithmic component of the [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) system that identifies handwritten or [cursive](/terms/cursive) characters, as opposed to printed or block characters.

Also referred to as the [connected recognizer](/terms/connected-recognizer).
