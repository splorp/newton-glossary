Title: Lantern

----

Text:

The driver development kit or [DDK](/terms/ddk) used to write a [Lantern Driver](/terms/lantern-driver). The [Lantern Library](/terms/lantern-library) or [API](/terms/api) is included in the [Newton Devices](/terms/newton-devices) package and is required in order to use any drivers you write with the DDK.
