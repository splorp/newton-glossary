Title: Revelar

----

Text:

A software development company based in Salt Lake City, UT. They created several products specifically for the Newton platform, including [Revelar Connection Utility](/terms/revelar-connection-utility), [Notable!](/terms/notable), and [Accordian](/terms/accordian).
