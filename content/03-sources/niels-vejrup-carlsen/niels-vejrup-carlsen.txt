Title: Niels Vejrup Carlsen

----

Date: 15 March 2018

----

Text:

Currently a partner at a Danish venture capital firm, Carlsen was a research engineer and project manager at [Siemens](https://www.siemens.com/) between 1993 and 1998. He was involved in the development of both the [Siemens Online Terminal](/terms/siemens-online-terminal) and [@net](/terms/atnet) devices.
