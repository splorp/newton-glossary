Title: Textware Solutions

Date: 21 November 2017

Text:

A software development company specializing in text input software for Windows, Newton, and Palm platforms. Textware products include the [Instant Text](http://www.fitaly.com/product/version7.htm) text expansion utility and the [Fitaly](/terms/fitaly) onscreen keyboard.
