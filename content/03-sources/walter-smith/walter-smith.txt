Title: Walter Smith

----

Date:

----

Text:

A developer and systems architect who joined [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) in 1988 as an intern on the Newton team. Before leaving Apple in 1996, Smith contributed several key technical components to the Newton, including [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript) and the persistent object store, better known as the [soup](/terms/soup).

Smith is also the current proprietor of the [Newton Museum](http://www.newtonmuseum.com/).
