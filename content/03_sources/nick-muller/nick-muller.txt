Title: Nick Müller

----

Date: 13 November 2017

----

Text:

Proprietor of [Logic Tools](https://web.archive.org/web/19990224041921/http://www.logictools.de/) and developer of several software titles for both Mac OS and (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). Müller was partly responsible for writing software for the (link: /terms/siemens-online-terminal text: Siemens Online Terminal) and was a founder of the Unabhängige Newton User Group München (UNUGMUC).
