Title: Avanti Software

----

Date: 15 April 2026

----

Text:

A software development company founded by Fredrik Holmen and Felix Hagno in 1996. Based in Sweden, Avanti created software for handheld computing platforms including (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). They were likely best known for QuickPress and the (link: /terms/shuffler text: Shuffler) bookmark utility.
