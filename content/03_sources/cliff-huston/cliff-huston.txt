Title: Cliff Huston

----

Date: 24 August 2020

----

Text:

One of the legendary Huston brothers, Apple employee #27, older brother to (link: /sources/dick-huston text: Dick). Known as an incredibly experienced, if somewhat cynical technician, Cliff designed the analogue board for the original Apple II disk drive, amongst other contributions to the platform.
