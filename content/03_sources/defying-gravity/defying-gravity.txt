Title: Defying Gravity: The Making of Newton

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Published in 1993, this book documents the creation of the Newton, focusing more on the people then the technology itself. Written by [Markos Kounalakis](https://www.markoskounalakis.com/) and featuring the dynamic black and white photography of [Doug Menuez](https://menuez.com/).
