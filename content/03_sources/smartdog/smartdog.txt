Title: Smart Dog Software

----

Date: 30 October 2001

----

Text:

A software development company founded by Robert Ellison. Smart Dog Software’s most popular product was (link: /terms/backtalk text: BackTalk), software which provides updated infrared communication protocols for the Newton platform. They also released several games for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), including a variation of _Settlers of Catan_.
