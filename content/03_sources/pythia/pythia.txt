Title: Pythia Communication & Computer GmbH

----

Date: 15 January 2026

----

Text:

A German software company that provided communication, system integration, and application development for handheld devices. Pythia was responsible for the (link: /terms/newton-data-viewer text: Newton Data Viewer) software that shipped with the (link: /terms/newton-connection-kit text: Newton Connection Kit) in Germany.
