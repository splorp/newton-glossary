Title: NS BASIC Corporation

----

Date: 06 July 2025

----

Text:

A software development company founded in 1993 by George Henne and based in Toronto, Ontario. Specializing in creating programming tools for handheld devices, including their namesake (link: /terms/ns-basic text: NS BASIC) and (link: /terms/newtcard text: NewtCard) products for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).

The company is now known simply as NSB Corporation.
