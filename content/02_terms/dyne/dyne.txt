Title: Dyne

----

Date: 21 July 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/prototype text: prototype)-based programming language inspired by (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) developed by (link: /sources/matthias-melcher text: Matthias Melcher).

Dyne is designed to maintain backward compatibility with traditional NewtonScript while extending the language, adding modern features, and providing 64-bit compatibility.

The Dyne language is part of the (link: /terms/dynelang text: DyneLang) project which also includes an (link: /terms/object text: object) system, (link: /terms/compiler text: compiler), (link: /terms/interpreter text: interpreter), and other tools.
