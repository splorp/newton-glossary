Title: Charge State

----

Date:

----

Text:

The current status of a rechargeable [battery](/terms/battery) in a Newton device. States can include Charging, Discharging, Trickle Charging, and [Deep Toast](/terms/deep-toast). The charge state of a battery can be observed using an [application](/terms/application) such as [NewtTest](/terms/newttest) or [SBM Utilities](/terms/sbm-utilities).
