Title: USB-001

----

Date: 15 February 2026

----

Text:

An internal circuit board for (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices developed by (link: /sources/newtonsales text: Newton Sales). When installed, it provides a standard (link: /terms/usb text: USB)-C port, eliminating the need for a (link: /terms/newton-serial-adapter text: Newton Serial Adapter) plugged into the proprietary (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect).

Two versions of the USB-001 board have been developed, both providing serial connectivity. A variation of the base model also provides the ability to power the Newton device through the USB port, as well as recharge the (link: /terms/battery-pack text: battery pack).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/dongle-destroyer text: Dongle Destroyer).
