Title: NewtCOM

----

Date: 28 April 2026

----

Text:

A multifunction (link: /terms/dongle text: dongle) developed by (link: /sources/matthias-melcher text: Matthias Melcher) that utilizes the (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton Interconnect) port to provide a (link: /terms/usb text: USB-C) port for serial data transfer and power.

Additionally, the NewtCOM has a (link: /terms/slot text: slot) for an SD card, which can contain (link: /terms/package-file text: package files), allowing installion of software directly from the dongle.

NewtCOM is also the name of an unspecified prototype that was being developed by (link: /sources/adriano-angelillis text: Adriano Angelillis).
