A system enhancement developed by (link: /sources/byte-size text: Byte-Size Software) that provides quick access for editing, navigating, and (link: /terms/highlighting text: highlighting) and (link: /terms/select text: selecting) text, entering punctuation and user-definable (link: /terms/character text: characters), accessing the (link: /terms/clipboard text: clipboard), as well as saving and retrieving (link: /terms/text text: text) clippings.

The tools are automatically displayed as (link: /terms/button text: buttons) in a (link: /terms/floating-palette text: floating palette) when text is (link: /terms/select text: selected).
