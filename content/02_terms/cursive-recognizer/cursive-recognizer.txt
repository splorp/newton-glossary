Title: Cursive Recognizer

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The algorithmic component of the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system that identifies handwritten or (link: /terms/cursive text: cursive) characters, as opposed to printed or block (link: /terms/character text: characters).

In prerelease versions of Newton OS 1.0, it is known as a (link: /terms/text-interpreter text: Text Interpreter).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/connected-recognizer text: Connected Recognizer), (link: /terms/paragraph-recognizer text: Paragraph Recognizer), and (link: /terms/text-recognizer text: Text Recognizer).
