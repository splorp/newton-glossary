Title: NE2000

----

Date: 01 August 2025

----

Text:

A widely adopted (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet) networking (link: /terms/card text: card) developed by [Novell](https://web.archive.org/web/20140101051318/http://www.novell.com/home/).

Using a specific (link: /terms/device-driver text: device driver) installed as an (link: /terms/extension text: extension), the (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) version of this card is compatible with certain Newton devices.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/ne2k text: NE2K).
