Title: Developer Signature

----

Date: 29 May 2026

----

Text:

A short, arbitrary sequence of (link: /terms/character text: characters) used to uniquely identify a creator or Newton developer. The signature is used to avoid naming conflicts with other (link: /terms/application text: applications) and (link: /terms/package text: packages) by facilitating the creation of unique (link: /terms/application-symbol text: application symbols), (link: /terms/soup text: soup) names, as well as other (link: /terms/global text: global) symbol) and strings.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/signature text: Signature).
