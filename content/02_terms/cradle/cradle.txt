Title: Cradle

----

Date: 25 March 2017

----

Text:

A stand or (link: /terms/docking-station text: docking station) designed to hold a device, usually integrating a means to charge the (link: /terms/battery text: battery). A cradle can also provide ports to connect the device to a (link: /terms/desktop-computer text: desktop computer), (link: /terms/laptop-computer text: laptop computer), or a network.

An example is the (link: /terms/charging-station text: charging station) manufactured by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) which was designed to work with the (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), and (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) devices.
