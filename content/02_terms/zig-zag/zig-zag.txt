Title: Zig-Zag

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A back and forth (link: /terms/gesture text: gesture) made with the (link: /terms/pen text: pen). This gesture is usually performed in order to (link: /terms/delete text: delete) text or (link: /terms/ink text: ink) drawings on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) of a Newton device.

Zig-zag can also describe a (link: /terms/line text: line) or other graphic element that alternates sharply in opposite directions.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/zigzag text: Zigzag).
