Title: Shapes

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

A [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) setting selected by [tapping](/terms/tapping) the [recognition button](/terms/recognition-button). When this setting is selected and the user draws a [shape](/terms/shape) on the Newton device, curves will be smoothed and lines will be straightened automatically.
