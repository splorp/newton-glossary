Title: Shapes

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) setting selected by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/recognition-button text: recognition button). When this setting is selected and the user draws a (link: /terms/shape text: shape) on a Newton device, curves will be smoothed and lines will be straightened automatically.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/shape-recognition text: Shape Recognition).
