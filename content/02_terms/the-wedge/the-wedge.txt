An accessory manufactured by (link: /sources/roadrunner-tracks text: RoadRunner Tracks) to hold a (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad) device at a slight angle when sitting on a desk or other work surface. The lightweight stand was made from transparent thermoplastic.
