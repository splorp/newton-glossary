Title: Font Engine

----

Date: 01 February 2026

----

Text:

Software that provides a mechanism for converting information from a digital (link: /terms/font text: font) into a (link: /terms/bitmap text: bitmap) image that can be displayed on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) of a Newton device.

(link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) has the capability to support third-party font engines.
