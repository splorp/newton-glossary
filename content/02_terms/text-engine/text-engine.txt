Title: Text Engine

----

Date: 16 December 2025

----

Text:

Software that provides a mechanism for creating, storing, manipulating, formatting, and otherwise editing a data structure consisting of (link: /terms/text text: text).

A text engine can include functionality to adjust various typographic characteristics such as (link: /terms/font-size text: font size), (link: /terms/font-style text: font style), line spacing, margins, paragraph indents, justification, and embedding (link: /terms/picture text: pictures) or other graphical elements within the text itself.

(link: /terms/newton works text: Newton Works) is an example of an (link: /terms/application text: application) that implements a text engine for its word processing feature.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/text-editing-engine text: Text-Editing Engine).
