Title: DyneSect

----

Date: 21 July 2026

----

Text:

A toolkit developed by (link: /sources/matthias-melcher text: Matthias Melcher) to “dissect, understand, and eventually modify” the (link: /terms/newton os text: Newton OS) ROM to better comprehend and troubleshoot (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) behaviour, potentially helping extend the lifespan of the Newton platform.

DyneSect reads (link: /terms/aif text: AIF) (ARM Image Format) and (link: /terms/rom-extension text: ROM Extension) (REx) images distributed with the (link: /terms/newton-c-tools text: Newton C++ Tools).

DyneSect is part of the (link: /terms/dynelang text: DyneLang) project which also includes the (link: /terms/Dyne text: Dyne) programming language, an (link: /terms/object text: object) system, (link: /terms/compiler text: compiler), (link: /terms/interpreter text: interpreter), and other tools.
