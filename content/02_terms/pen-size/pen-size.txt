Title: Pen Size

----

Date: 25 June 2026

----

Text:

A section of the (link: /terms/pen-tool text: pen tool) palette found in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works).

(image: /assets/images/pen-tool-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Pen Tool palette in Newton Works width: 200)

Along with a (link: /terms/pen-shade text: Pen Shade) section, the (link: /terms/palette text: palette) contains various widths of line that can be applied to (link: /terms/line text: lines) and edges of (link: /terms/object text: objects) in a (link: /terms/drawing text: drawing) document.

This is similar to selecting a pen size in the (link: /terms/styles text: Styles) application.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/pen-pattern text: Pen Width).
