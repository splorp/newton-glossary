Title: Receive Key

----

Date: 13 December 2025

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/function-key text: function key) located on the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) keyboard that initiates the process of receiving (link: /terms/beaming text: beamed) items from another Newton device.

Pressing the Receive key will open a (link: /terms/slip text: slip) with the message “Looking for sender”. When another user presses the (link: /terms/send-key text: Send key) on an eMate or uses the (link: /terms/routing-button text: routing button) to beam an item on Newton device, the slip will disappear.

Once received, the beamed item will be listed in the (link: /terms/in-box text: In Box).
