Text:

A (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) setting selected by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/recognition-button text: recognition button). When this setting is selected, words and numbers written on a Newton device will be converted to (link: /terms/recognized-text text: recognized text).

Text also refers to any data or file format that consists of human-readable, alphanumeric (link: /terms/character text: characters).
