Title: nbk

----

Date:

----

Text:

The file extension used to identify [backup files](/terms/backup-file) created by the Windows versions of the [Newton Backup Utility](/terms/newton-backup-utility) and [Newton Connection Utilities](/terms/newton-connection-utilities) applications.
