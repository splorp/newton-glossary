Title: Meeting

----

Date: 25 June 2001

----

Text:

An entry in the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) application for a specific time during the day. People can be invited and the meeting can be scheduled for a particular location.

The time and duration of the meeting can be changed by (link: /terms/dragging text: dragging) the vertical (link: /terms/marker text: marker) associated with the meeting.
