Title: Craft Access System

----

Date: 05 April 2026

----

Text:

A communication network developed in 1984 by a subsidiary division of AT&T that allowed telephone installers and repair personnel to access work order information using a handheld device connected to the landline telephone system.

The (link: /terms/supertech-2000 text: SuperTech 2000) was a (link: /terms/craft-digital-assistant text: craft digital assistant) based on the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) platform that could utilize this system.
