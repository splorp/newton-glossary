Title: Gravity

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A drawing feature in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) that causes the ends of a newly drawn (link: /terms/line text: line) to snap to nearby corners or midpoints of existing graphic (link: /terms/shape text: shapes).
