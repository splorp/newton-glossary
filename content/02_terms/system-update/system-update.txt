Title: System Update

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A small software [patch](/terms/patch) that is installed on a Newton device to modify or update certain aspects of the [ROM](/terms/rom)-based [operating system](/terms/operating-system).

Also referred to as a [System Patch](/terms/system-patch).
