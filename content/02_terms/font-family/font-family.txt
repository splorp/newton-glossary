Title: Font Family

----

Date: 04 February 2026

----

Text:

A group of several individual (link: /terms/font text: fonts) that are related by an overall design or aesthetic. A font family generally includes various (link: /terms/font-style text: font styles), such as regular, italic, bold, and bold italic.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/font-style text: Typeface).
