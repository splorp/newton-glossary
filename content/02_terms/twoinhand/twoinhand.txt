Title: TwoInHand

----

Date: 23 April 2026

----

Text:

An [Apple II](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_II) emulator for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) rumoured to have been in development by (link: /sources/peter-liethen text: Peter Liethen). Although it was never released, TwoInHand purportedly emulated a 128K Apple IIc computer equipped with dual floppy drives, as well as (link: /terms/modem text: modem) and printer ports.
