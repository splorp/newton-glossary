Title: Framed Data

----

Date: 14 February 2026

----

Text:

Information that has been formed into predetermined (link: /terms/frame text: frames) according to a specific (link: /terms/protocol text: protocol), generally for transfer over a network or between devices.

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), the (link: /terms/global text: global) `Translate` (link: /terms/function text: function) allows data to be converted to a (link: /terms/flattened-frame text: flattened frame) and (link: /terms/packetized text: packetized) by using the (link: /terms/target text: target) frame in the (link: /terms/output-spec text: output spec).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/packetized-data text: Packetized Data).
