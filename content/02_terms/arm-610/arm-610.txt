Title: ARM 610

----

Date: 03 June 2026

----

Text:

The low-power (link: /terms/risc text: RISC) processor used in Newton devices, developed by (link: /terms/arm text: ARM). It was selected to replace the (link: /terms/hobbit text: Hobbit) processor developed by AT&T.

The (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad), (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100), (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130), Sharp (link: /terms/expertpad text: ExpertPad), Siemens (link: /terms/notephone text: NotePhone), Harris (link: /terms/supertech-2000 text: SuperTech 2000), Digital Ocean (link: /terms/tarpon text: Tarpon), and Digital Ocean (link: /terms/seahorse text: Seahorse) all use a 20 MHz ARM 610 processor.
