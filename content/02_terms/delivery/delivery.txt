Desktop Connection Library

An (link: /terms/application text: application) created by (link: /sources/paul-guyot text: Paul Guyot) for installing (link: /terms/package text: packages) on a Newton device over (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk) and (link: /terms/tcp-ip text: TCP/IP) using Mac OS X.

Delivery is based on the (link: /terms/desktop-connection-library text: Desktop Connection Library) framework and implements the (link: /terms/bonjour text: Bonjour) (also known as (link: /terms/rendezvous text: Rendezvous)) networking service.
