Title: PALMEDIA Image Transfer

----

Date: 14 May 2026

----

Text:

A [HyperCard](https://hypercard.org/) stack developed by (link: /sources/lee-moon text: Lee Moon) that uploaded images to a Newton device using Steve Weyer’s (link: /terms/sloup text: Sloup) utility.

The images could then be viewed using the (link: /terms/palmedia-player text: PALMEDIA Player) application.

(image: /assets/images/palmedia-image-transfer-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the PALMEDIA Image Transfer stack width: 461)

A later version of the stack called (link: /terms/palmedia-media-transfer text: PALMEDIA Media Transfer) supported additional media types, including sound and movie files.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/pit text: PIT).
