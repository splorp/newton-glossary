----

Date: 10 July 2026

Text:

Wireless Wide Area Network

A data communications network that provides connectivity to wireless devices over a large geographical area using cellular, radio, or other mobile communication technologoes.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/wireless-wan text: Wireless WAN).

Compare with a (link: /terms/wlan text: WLAN) (Wireless Local Area Network) which covers a small area, usually over (link: /terms/wi-fi text: Wi-Fi).
