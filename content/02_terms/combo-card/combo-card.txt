Title: Combo Card

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) that combines the functionality of two separate cards. For example, a (link: /terms/modem text: modem) and an (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet) networking card.
