Title: Floating Bar

----

Date: 28 May 2026

----

Text:

A type of (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) that floats at the bottom of an item (link: /terms/view text: view) within an (link: /terms/application text: application).

(image: /assets/images/floating-bar-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of a Floating Bar containing outline entry buttons width: 106)

For example, an (link: /terms/outline text: outline) item in the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application has a set of (link: /terms/button text: buttons) that are used for adding new outline entries, but are not used to interact with the main application (link: /terms/view text: view).
