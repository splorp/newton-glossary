Title: PALMEDIA Media Transfer

----

Date: 14 May 2026

----

Text:

A [HyperCard](https://hypercard.org/) stack developed by (link: /sources/lee-moon text: Lee Moon) that converted and uploaded images, sounds, and movie files to a Newton device using Steve Weyer’s (link: /terms/sloup text: Sloup) utility.

The media could then be viewed using the (link: /terms/palmedia-player text: PALMEDIA Player) application.

(image: /assets/images/palmedia-media-transfer-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the PALMEDIA Media Transfer stack width: 461)

An earlier version of the stack called (link: /terms/palmedia-image-transfer text: PALMEDIA Image Transfer) was limited to handling images only.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/pmt text: PMT).
