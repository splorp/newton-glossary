Title: QuickFlight

----

Date: 09 July 2026

----

Text:

A suite of client and server software technologies developed by (link: /sources/ete text: ETE) providing wireless communication and data delivery across multiple computing platforms.

Used in combination with a wireless networking service such as (link: /terms/ardis text: ARDIS), QuickFlight used a (link: /terms/transport text: transport) integrated into (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), allowing nearly any (link: /terms/application text: application) to send and receive data via the built-in (link: /terms/routing text: routing) mechanism.

QuickFlight also simplified the use of a GPS receiver with a Newton device.
