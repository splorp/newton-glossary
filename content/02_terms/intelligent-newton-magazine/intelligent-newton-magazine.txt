Title: Intelligent Newton Magazine

----

Date: 09 March 2026

----

Text:

A bimonthly magazine published between 1994 and 1995 by INHand Publishing and edited by Barry Owen. Also known as (link: /terms/in-magazine text: IN Magazine) (or simply (link: /terms/in text: IN)), it billed itself as _“The information source for intelligent Newton users”_. It ceased publication after only six issues.

Intelligent Newton was known for its regular coverage of Newton news, software reviews, and product information. The magazine provided an extensive amount of information for the (link: /terms/newton-resource-guide text: Newton Resource Guide) distributed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/).
