Title: Soup

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In [NewtonScript](/terms/newtonscript), a persistently stored [object](/terms/object) that contains a series of [frames](/terms/frame) called [entries](/terms/entry). Like a [database](/terms/database), a soup has indexes that are used to access entries in a sorted order. One aspect of a soup that sets it apart from an object oriented database is the “smart” [flattening](/terms/flattening) of entries before they are added to the soup.
