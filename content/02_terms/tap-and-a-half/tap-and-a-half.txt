Title: Tap-and-a-Half

----

Date:

----

Text:

To [tap](/terms/tap), then quickly lift, touch, and hold the [pen](/terms/pen) in the same spot on the [screen](/terms/screen). Essentially performing a down, up, down action with the pen.
