Title: Modifier Key

----

Date: 24 January 2018

----

Text:

A key that affects the function of the alphanumeric keys on a keyboard. The use of a modifier key in conjunction with another key is known as a (link: /terms/key-combination text: key combination). Modifier keys include (link: /terms/caps-lock-key text: Caps Lock), (link: /terms/command-key text: Command), (link: /terms/control-key text: Control), (link: /terms/shift-key text: Shift), and (link: /terms/option-key text: Option).
