Title: RJ-45

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A standard modular connector, used for [ethernet](/terms/ethernet) connections. Similar to the [RJ-11](/terms/rj-45) modular connector used for telephone and [AppleTalk](/terms/appletalk) connections.
