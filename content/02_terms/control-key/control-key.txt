Title: Control Key

----

Date: 22 March 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/modifier-key text: modifier key) used to produce standard, non-printing ASCII (link: /terms/character text: characters), primarily in a terminal emulation (link: /terms/application text: application). In (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), characters modified using the Control key are not inserted into text (link: /terms/input-area text: input areas), such as the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application.

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/ctrl-key text: Ctrl Key).
