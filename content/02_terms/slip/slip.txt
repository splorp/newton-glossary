Title: Slip

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) with a (link: /terms/matte-border text: matte border) displayed by an (link: /terms/application text: application) in order to obtain user (link: /terms/input text: input), or to present alternatives which a user can choose to determine the outcome of a task that has been started.

In comparison, a slip is similar to a dialog box on a Mac OS or Windows computer.

For the protocol, see (link: /terms/slip-protocol text: SLIP).
