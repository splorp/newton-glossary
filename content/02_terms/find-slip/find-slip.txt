Title: Find Slip

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

An input (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) that allows the user to (link: /terms/enter text: enter) a term or phrase to be located within the data stored on a Newton device.

(image: /assets/images/find-slip-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Find Slip width: 320)

The standard Find slip contains a labeled (link: /terms/input-line text: input line) used to specify a term or phrase and several (link: /terms/radio-button text: radio buttons) used to specify the scope of the search.
