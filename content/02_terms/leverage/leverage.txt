Title: Leverage

----

Date: 04 November 2025

Text:

A powerful, programmable (link: /terms/database text: database) application for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by (link: /sources/balcones text: Balcones Software). Features included easy creation of databases and queries, data exchange with desktop (link: /terms/application text: applications) such as [FileMaker Pro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FileMaker) and [Microsoft Access](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Access), support for (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery), relational split screen viewing, and flexible printing options.
