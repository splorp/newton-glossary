Title: Gesture Recognizer

----

Date: 28 January 2026

----

Text:

The algorithmic component of the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system that identifies a common set of (link: /terms/gesture text: gestures) made using the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) for corrections and editing. These recognized gestures include (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping), (link: /terms/scrubbing text: scrubbing), and drawing of the (link: /terms/caret text: caret).
