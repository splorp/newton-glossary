Title: Newton Resource Guide

----

Date: 09 March 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/database text: database) containing product and service information related to the Newton platform.

Created using FileMaker, the database was developed for [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) by (link: /sources/woodware text: WoodWare), an independent consultancy operated by Glenn Nunez. Using information provided by (link: /terms/intelligent-newton-magazine text: Intelligent Newton Magazine), the database featured nearly 800 informational listings covering software titles, peripherals, accessories, services, (link: /terms/newton-books text: Newton books), and other resources. These listings were published in the last issue of the magazine in 1995.

The Newton Resource Guide was also included as part of the (link: /terms/newton-software-datenbank text: Newton Software Datenbank) distributed by [Apple Computer GmbH](https://web.archive.org/web/19961221203435/http://www.apple.de/).

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nrg text: NRG).
