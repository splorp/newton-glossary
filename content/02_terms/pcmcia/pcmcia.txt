Title: PCMCIA

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Personal Computer Memory Card International Association

An international organization founded in 1989 to establish standards for integrated circuit cards and to promote interchangeability among mobile computing platforms.

Colloquially referred to as _People Can’t Remember Computer Industry Acronyms_ because of its rather lengthy nature.

PCMCIA is also used to describe the type of removable (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC cards) used by Newton devices for storage, connectivity, and other peripherals.

The Newton platform includes support for a superset of the PCMCIA standard called (link: /terms/trimbus text: TrimBus).
