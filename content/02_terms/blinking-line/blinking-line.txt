Title: Blinking Line

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A flashing vertical line indicating where new text will be inserted. More commonly referred to as a [cursor](/terms/cursor). The blinking line is only used in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 1.3 and earlier. Later versions of Newton OS use a [caret](/terms/caret) cursor to indicate where new text will be inserted.
