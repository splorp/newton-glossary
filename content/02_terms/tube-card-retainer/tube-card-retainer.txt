Title: Tube Card Retainer

----

Date: 03 April 2026

----

Text:

A loop located inside the (link: /terms/newt-boot text: Newt Boot) carrying case designed to hold a “Tube Card”, a fictitious item described as being “… about the size and shape of a pencil or pen.” Needless to say, the two “retainers” are actually for holding writing implements, not (link: /terms/card text: cards).
