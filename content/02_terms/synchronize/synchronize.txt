Title: Synchronize

----

Date:

----

Text:

A process where the data stored in two different locations is compared and updated so that both copies end up the same. For example, [Newton Connection Utilities](/terms/newton-connection-utilities) can synchronize data between a Newton device and a [desktop computer](/terms/desktop-computer) or [laptop](/terms/laptop-computer).
