Title: Heap

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The area of [system memory](/terms/system-memory) reserved for use by program functions and executable code. On a Newton device, heap often refers specifically to the [NewtonScript heap](/terms/newtonscript-heap) and [C++ heap](/terms/c-heap).

Also referred to as [Frames Heap](/terms/frames-heap).
