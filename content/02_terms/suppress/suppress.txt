Title: Suppress

----

Date: 15 April 2026

----

Text:

To deactivate an (link: /terms/application text: application) or (link: /terms/extension text: extension) package temporarily.

In (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.0 and later, it is possible for the user to suppress (link: /terms/package text: package) activation.

To suppress activation of packages on the (link: /terms/internal-store text: internal store), press the (link: /terms/reset-button text: reset button), then turn the Newton device over, and hold the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) down somewhere in the left quarter inch of the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

(image: /assets/images/activate-packages-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of an alert box asking “Do you want to activate the packages on the internet store?” width: 208)

An (link: /terms/alert-box text: alert box) will appear, which lets the user to chose whether to activate packages on the internal store.
