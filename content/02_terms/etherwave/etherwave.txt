Title: EtherWave

----

Date: 19 May 2026

----

Text:

A family of (link: /terms/network-interface-cards text: network interface cards) and adpaters developed by [Farallon Communications](https://web.archive.org/web/19980524075616/http://www.farallon.com/) that provided a link between devices that were limited to serial connections and (link: /terms/ethernet text: ethernet) networks, without the need of a dedicated (link: /terms/localtalk text: LocalTalk) bridge.

EtherWave hardware could be daisy-chained to other EtherWave or ethernet-enabled devices, creating a network without a central hub.

Farallon manufactured an EtherWave (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) and a (link: /terms/device-driver text: device driver) compatible with Newton devices.
