Title: Priority Button

----

Date: 26 April 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/picture-button text: picture button) associated with a (link: /terms/to-do-task text: to do task) that indicates the level of importance assigned to that (link: /terms/task text: task).

A task can be assigned a priority of 1, 2, 3, or ! (None).

To change the (link: /terms/priority text: priority) level of a (link: /terms/to-do-list text: To Do List) task, (link: /terms/tap text: tap) the picture button and (link: /terms/drag text: drag) it.

Dragging the task above a higher priority item gives it a higher priority.

Dragging the task below a lower priority item gives it a lower priority.

The priority of task can also be changed by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the priority button to open a (link: /terms/slip text: slip) where a new priority level can be selected using the (link: /terms/priority-picker text: priority picker).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/priority-marker text: Priority Marker).
