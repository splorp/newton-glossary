Title: NewtCard

----

Date: 14 January 2026

----

Text:

An development (link: /terms/application text: application) for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) similar to Apple’s <a href="https://hypercard.org/">HyperCard</a>. NewtCard allows the user to build a navigable “stack” of “cards” containing any combination of (link: /terms/text text: text), (link: /terms/shape text: shape) drawings, (link: /terms/bitmap text: bitmap) images, (link: /terms/field text: fields), (link: /terms/button text: buttons), and sounds. (link: /terms/script text: Scripts) written in a subset of (link: /terms/ns-basic text: NS BASIC) can be attached to stack, card, backgrounds, fields, and buttons.

NewtCard was developed by the <a href="https://www.nsbasic.com/">NS BASIC Corporation</a> based in Toronto.
