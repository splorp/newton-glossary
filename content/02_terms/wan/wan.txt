Wide Area Network

A data communications network that covers a large geographical area. A WAN typically consists of serial connections over physical copper or fibre-optic transmission lines over long distances. A wireless wide area network is known as a (link: /terms/wwan text: WWAN).

Compare with a (link: /terms/lan text: LAN) (Local Area Network) which covers a small or geographically limited area.
