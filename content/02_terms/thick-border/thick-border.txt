Title: Thick Border

----

Date: 14 June 2026

----

Text:

A black border that surrounds a selected (link: /terms/icon text: icon) or (link: /terms/picture text: picture) in a (link: /terms/picture-radio-button text: picture radio button) group. For example, the (link: /terms/card-style text: Card Style) slip uses a thick border to indicate the selected style.
