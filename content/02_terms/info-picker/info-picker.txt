Title: Info Picker

----

Date: 29 December 2017

----

Text:

A list of items pertaining to the current [application](/terms/application) that can include [preferences](/terms/preferences), onscreen help, or general information about the application. Activated by [tapping](/terms/tapping) on the [info button](/terms/info-button).
