Title: BO-A300

----

Date: 05 July 2026

----

Text:

The model number used in an early service manual to identify the first Newton devices that (link: /sources/sharp text: Sharp Corporation) manufactured for [Apple](https://www.apple.com/).

The (link: /terms/original-messagepad text: Original MessagePad) and (link: /terms/messagepad-100 text: MessagePad 100) are more commonly known by the (link: /terms/h1000 text: H1000) designation.
