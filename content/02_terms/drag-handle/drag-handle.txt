Title: Drag Handle

----

Date:

----

Text:

A small control that a user can [drag](/terms/drag) to move a [container view](/terms/container-view) such as a [palette](/terms/palette) or floating window. The drag handle is indicated by a small black tab with a central hole, centered on the top border of the container view.

Also referred to as a [Picture Hanger](/terms/picture-hanger).
