Title: ERS

----

Date: 18 January 2024

----

Text:

Engineering Requirements Specification

A shorthand term produced by Apple’s product marketing team that represented the defining of features required by the end user of a Newton product.

ERS is mentioned in the [Newton OS 2.1 Engineering Documents](https://www.newted.org/download/manuals/NewtonOS21EngDoc1997.pdf) and the [Newton Font Specifications](https://www.newted.org/download/manuals/NewtonFontSpecifications.pdf).
