Title: Toggle

----

Date: 28 January 2026

----

Text:

To switch or alternate between two states by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) a (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element such as a (link: /terms/button text: button).

For example, tapping a (link: /terms/checkbox text: checkbox) will toggle between a checked or unchecked state. Likewise, tapping the (link: /terms/overview-button text: overview button) will switch between two levels of detail, such as information for an individual item or a list of multiple items.
