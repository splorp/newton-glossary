Title: Jellyfish

----

Date: 27 October 2016

----

Text:

The purported code name for the clear (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110) device. Often mistaken for a (link: /terms/prototype text: prototype), the clear-cased version was a special edition of the MessagePad 110 made available to developers and [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) employees.
