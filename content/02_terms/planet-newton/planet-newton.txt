Title: Planet Newton

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A defunct website managed by Laura Painter featuring Newton-related news, resources, and a polpular annotated software archive. It was also home to the second iteration of the (link: /terms/newtontalk text: NewtonTalk) mailing list. Planet Newton was one of several websites developed and operated by media company Ideacast.
