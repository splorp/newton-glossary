Text:

A small elliptical (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element used to denote a (link: /terms/radio-button text: radio button), (link: /terms/checklist text: checklist) item, or (link: /terms/outline text: outline) item. In checklists and outlines, these elements are also referred to as (link: /terms/topic-marker text: topic markers).

A circle is also an elliptical (link: /terms/shape text: shape) similar to an (link: /terms/oval text: oval), but with all points along its edge equidistant from its centre.
