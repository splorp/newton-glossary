Title: Ribbon Cable

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

A flat, multiconductor cable used to connect electronic components or circuit boards to each other. For example, a ribbon cable is used to connect the [screen](/terms/screen) in a Newton device to the [motherboard](/terms/motherboard).
