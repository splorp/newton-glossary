Title: The Message

----

Date: 27 June 2026

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/david-fedor text: David Fedor) that allows the user to read and search the Bible on a Newton device. The software was released for multiple computing platforms as _MyBible_ and was also available for Palm OS devices as _Scripture_.
