Title: PALMEDIA Player

----

Date: 14 May 2026

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/lee-moon text: Lee Moon) that allowed viewing of images, animations, and movies on a Newton device. The software extended the features found in Moon’s earlier (link: /terms/newton-slide-show text: Newton Slide Show) and (link: /terms/newton-movie-player text: Newton Movie Player) applications.

Using a [HyperCard](https://hypercard.org/) stack called (link: /terms/palmedia-image-transfer text: PALMEDIA Image Transfer) (later renamed to (link: /terms/palmedia-media-transfer text: PALMEDIA Media Transfer)) you could easily upload files from a Mac OS computer to a Newton device for use with PALMEDIA Player.

A version of the player called (link: /terms/palmedia-player-2000 text: PALMEDIA Player 2000) was released specifically for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.1 devices.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/pmp text: PMP), (link: /terms/pmplay text: PMPlay), (link: /terms/pmplayer text: PMPlayer), and (link: /terms/player2k text: Player2K).
