Title: Font

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A complete set of (link: /terms/character text: characters) or glyphs from a given (link: /terms/typeface text: typeface) design supplied in a specific physical or digital format.

For example, (link: /terms/system text: System) is a typeface or (link: /terms/font-family text: font family) included with (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) that is comprised of several (link: /terms/font-size text: font sizes) and (link: /terms/font-style text: font styles).

Whereas System is the typeface, the 12 (link: /terms/point text: point) variation of System Bold is an individual font within the System family.

The terms font and typeface are often used interchangeably.
