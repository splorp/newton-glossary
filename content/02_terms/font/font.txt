Title: Font

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A complete set of (link: /terms/character text: characters) or glyphs from a given typeface design. For example, (link: /terms/system text: System) is a (link: /terms/bitmap text: bitmap) typeface included with (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS), but 12 (link: /terms/point text: point) System Bold is a specific (link: /terms/font-style text: font style) of that typeface.
