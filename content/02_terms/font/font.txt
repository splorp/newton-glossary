Title: Font

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A complete set of characters or glyphs from a given typeface design. For example, [System](/terms/system) is a typeface included with the [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os), but 12 [point](/terms/point) System Bold is a specific font of that typeface.
