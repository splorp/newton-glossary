Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) created by (link: /sources/daniel-padilla-roldan text: Daniel Padilla Roldán) that can control iPod models with an [iPod Remote](https://apple.fandom.com/wiki/IPod_Remote) port using a Newton device and a custom (link: /terms/newton-interconnect text: Newton InterConnect) cable such as Adriano Angelillis’ (link: /terms/NewtPod text: NewtPod).
