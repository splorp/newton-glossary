Title: Bounding Box

----

Date: 17 February 2026

----

Text:

The smallest (link: /terms/rectangle text: rectangle) that completely surrounds or contains an (link: /terms/object text: object).

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a bounding box defines the shape of the (link: /terms/region text: region) containing a (link: /terms/view text: view) or other data. This includes a value for the left, right, top, and bottom of the region in (link: /terms/global text: global) coordinates.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/bounding-rectangle text: Bounding Rectangle).
