Title: Flatten

----

Date: 21 November 2017

----

Text:

To convert a frame [object](/terms/object) into a stream of bytes before an [entry](/terms/entry) is added to a [soup](/terms/soup). To reverse this process, you would [unflatten](/terms/unflatten) the soup entry.
