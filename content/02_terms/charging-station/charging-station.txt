Title: Charging Station

----

Date: 23 March 2026

----

Text:

A hardware accessory manufactured by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that provided a (link: /terms/cradle text: cradle) to hold a (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), or (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) device.

The charging station featured a (link: /terms/pen-holder text: pen holder) and could simultaneously charge the (link: /terms/battery-pack text: battery pack) in the (link: /terms/messagepad text: MessagePad), as well as another pack inserted into the station itself.

The code name of the charging station was (link: /terms/crib text: Crib).
