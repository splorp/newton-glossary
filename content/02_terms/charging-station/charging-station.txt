Title: Charging Station

----

Date: 23 March 2026

----

Text:

A hardware accessory manufactured by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that provided a (link: /terms/cradle text: cradle) to hold a (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110), (link: /terms/messagepad-120 text: MessagePad 120), or (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130) device, a (link: /terms/pen-holder text: pen holder), and the means to recharge the (link: /terms/battery-pack text: battery pack).

Using the charging station, two battery packs could be recharged at the same time - one pack in the MessagePad set on the cradle and one pack in the charging station itself.

The code name of the charging station was (link: /terms/crib text: Crib).
