Title: Wi-Fi

----

Date: 08 November 2025

----

Text:

A common wireless local area networking ((link: /terms/wlan text: WLAN)) technology that implements (link: /terms/protocol text: protocols) based on the (link: /terms/802-11 text: 802.11) standard.

Certain Newton devices can connect to a Wi-Fi network using a wireless (link: /terms/network-interface-card text: network interface card) and installing the appropriate (link: /terms/device-driver text: device driver).
