Title: Enumerate

----

Date: 28 May 2026

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), to count or list a number of items one by one.

For example, the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) uses an (link: /terms/enumerated-dictionary text: enumerated dictionary) containing a list of strings that are compared to (link: /terms/user-input text: user input).

Also, when creating certain (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) elements, such as a (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar), a programmer can enumerate the order in which the (link: /terms/button text: buttons) to appear on the bar.
