Title: Newton Communications Architecture

----

Date: 23 July 2025

----

Text:

A marketing-focused term for the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) technologies that provide built-in support for various serial communication and internet (link: /terms/protocol text: protocols), (link: /terms/modem text: modems), sending and receiving faxes, email, and connection of an external keyboard.

Due to its modular design, the architecture was able to support additional communication capabilities as they became available such as (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet) and wireless networking.
