Title: ASIC

----

Date: 24 May 2001

Text:

Application Specific Integrated Circuit

A custom [VLSI](/terms/vlsi) computer chip or [chipset](/terms/chipset) designed to perform one or more particular functions. Advantages include fewer discrete components, lower power consumption, and increased reliability.
