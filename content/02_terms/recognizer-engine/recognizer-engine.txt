Text:

Software that provides a mechanism for the conversion of (link: /terms/character text: characters), words, (link: /terms/shape text: shapes), (link: /terms/sketch text: sketches), and (link: /terms/gesture text: gestures) drawn with a (link: /terms/pen text: pen) on a (link: /terms/screen text: screen) into digital data that can be corrected, edited, modified, or otherwise parsed by the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system).

(link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) has the capability to support third-party (link: /terms/recognizers text: recognizers), not just those built into the platform.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/recognizer text: Recognizer).
