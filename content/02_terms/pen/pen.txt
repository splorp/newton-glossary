Title: Pen

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The handheld instrument used to (link: /terms/enter text: enter) data into a Newton device via the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/stylus text: Stylus).

Pen is also a setting in the (link: /terms/styles text: Styles) application which allows the user to select the (link: /terms/pen-size text: pen size) that is used when writing or drawing on the screen.
