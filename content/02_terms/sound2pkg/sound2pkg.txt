Title: Sound2Pkg

----

Date: 11 December 2025

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/nobuo-kawaguchi text: Nobuo Kawaguchi) that purportedly could convert Mac OS sound files to Newton (link: /terms/package text: packages), which can then be installed and played on a Newton device. It is not known if the software was actually released publicly.
