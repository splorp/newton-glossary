Title: Mobile Information Management

----

Date: 15 January 2026

----

Text:

A term used to describe the capabilities of the (link: /terms/newton-data-viewer text: Newton Data Viewer) software, which involved downloading data from desktop (link: /terms/application text: applications) to a Newton device for viewing, modifying, and (link: /terms/synchronizing text: synchronizing) changes back to a desktop computer.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/mim text: MIM).
