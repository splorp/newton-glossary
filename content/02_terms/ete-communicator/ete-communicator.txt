Title: ETE Communicator

----

Date: 09 July 2026

----

Text:

A hardware accessory developed by (link: /sources/ete text: ETE) that transformed a Newton device into a wireless communication and navigation server.

The hardware consisted of a lightweight (link: /terms/cradle text: cradle) designed to hold an (link: /terms/expertpad text: ExpertPad). A cable connected the cradle to the (link: /terms/serial-port text: serial port) of the ExpertPad, providing access to multiple networking systems via (link: /terms/modem text: modem), GPS, (link: /terms/packet text: packet) radio, and cellular.
