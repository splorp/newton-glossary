Title: Guest User

----

Date: 05 September 2017

----

Text:

An option in the [handwriting recognition](/terms/handwriting-recognition) preferences which provides temporary settings for another user. When selected, any [preferences](/terms/preferences) changed by the guest user will not affect the preferences previously set by the [owner](/terms/owner) of the Newton device.

Also referred to as [guest mode](/terms/guest-mode).
