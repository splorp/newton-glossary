Title: Font Style

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A stylistic variation pertaining to a (link: /terms/font text: font), other than its (link: /terms/font-family text: font family) or (link: /terms/point text: point) size.

Examples of font styles include regular, italic, bold, and underline.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/font-face text: Font Face).
