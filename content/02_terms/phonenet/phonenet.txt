Title: PhoneNET

----

Date: 23 June 2024

----

Text:

An implementation of (link: /terms/appletalk text: AppleTalk) networking developed by Farallon Communications. PhoneNET used (link: /terms/mini-din text: Mini-DIN) connectors to connect to (link: /terms/localtalk text: LocalTalk) ports found on printers and Mac OS computers combined with standard (link: /terms/rj-11 text: RJ-11) jacks and four-conductor telephone wire, enabling it share the same cable used by existing telephone wiring.
