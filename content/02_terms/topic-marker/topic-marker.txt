Title: Topic Marker

----

Date: 18 September 2024

----

Text:

A small circular indicator displayed next to each topic in a (link: /terms/structured-list text: structured list) such as an (link: /terms/outline text: outline) or (link: /terms/checklist text: checklist). A topic can be moved above or below another topic by (link: /terms/dragging text: dragging) the topic marker.

Topic markers are also used in the (link: /terms/newton-press text: Newton Press) application to indicate where table of contents items in a (link: /terms/newton-book text: Newton book) are linked.
