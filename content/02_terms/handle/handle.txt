Title: Handle

----

Date: 17 February 2026

----

Text:

A small (link: /terms/square text: square) that appears on each corner of a (link: /terms/selection-box text: selection box) when an (link: /terms/object text: object) is (link: /terms/selected text: selected). By (link: /terms/dragging text: dragging) ones of the handles, the object can be stretched, scaled, or distorted.

The (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) also features a physical handle to carry the device.
