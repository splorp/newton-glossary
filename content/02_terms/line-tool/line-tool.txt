Title: Line Tool

----

Date: 17 June 2026

----

Text:

A tool found in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) used to add straight line (link: /terms/object text: objects) to a (link: /terms/drawing text: drawing) document.

(link: /terms/dragging text: Dragging) the line tool on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) using the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) will create a (link: /terms/line text: line) in the direction of the (link: /terms/drag text: drag). To create a vertical, horizontal, or 45° diagonal line, hold down the (link: /terms/shift-key text: Shift key) while dragging the tool.

Tapping on the line with the (link: /terms/pointer-tool text: pointer tool) will cause (link: /terms/handle text: handles) to appear at each end of the line, allowing the user to move or resize the object.

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/straight-line-tool text: Straight-Line Tool).
