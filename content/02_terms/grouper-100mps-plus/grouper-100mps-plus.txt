Title: Grouper 100MPS+

----

Date: 01 October 2017

----

Text:

A member of the (link: /terms/grouper text: Grouper) family of devices developed by (link: /sources/digital-ocean text: Digital Ocean), this radio-based peripheral provided (link: /terms/wireless-appletalk text: wireless AppleTalk) connectivity over a (link: /terms/lan text: LAN). Identical in functionality to the (link: /terms/grouper-100mps text: Grouper 100MPS), with additional hardware support for external data collection and (link: /terms/output text: output), such as barcode scanners, magnetic card readers, printers, and other serial devices with a (link: /terms/mini-din text: Mini-DIN) connector.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/100mps-plus text: 100MPS+).
