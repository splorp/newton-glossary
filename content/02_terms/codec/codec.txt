Title: Codec

----

Date: 23 March 2017

----

Text:

Hardware or software that converts analogue information into its digital equivalent or digital information into its analogue equivalent. (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.1 includes codecs for muLaw, IMA, (link: /terms/gsm text: GSM), and a synthesizer codec for sound generation. The (link: /terms/macintalk text: MacinTalk) extension provides an additional codec which converts (link: /terms/recognized-text text: recognized text) into synthesized speech.

Codec is acronym of “coder-decoder” or “compressor-decompressor” depending on the application.
