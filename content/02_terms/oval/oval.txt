Title: Oval

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

An elliptical (link: /terms/shape text: shape) defined by the (link: /terms/bounding-box text: bounding box) that encloses it.

Whereas a (link: /terms/circle text: circle) has all points along its edge equidistant from its centre, the points along the edge of an oval are non-equidistant from its centre.
