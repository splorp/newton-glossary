Title: StrongARM

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The low-power (link: /terms/risc text: RISC) processor used in later Newton devices, developed by (link: /terms/arm text: ARM) in conjunction with [Digital Equipment Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Equipment_Corporation).

The (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100), Harris (link: /terms/access-device-2000 text: Access Device 2000), and Schlumberger (link: /terms/watson text: Watson) all use a 162 MHz StrongARM (link: /terms/sa-110 text: SA-110) processor.
