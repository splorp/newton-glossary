Title: LunaSuite

----

Date: 15 May 2026

----

Text:

A set of modular internet (link: /terms/application text: applications) developed by (link: /sources/lunatech text: Lunatech Research) that took advantage of technologies available in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) 2.1. The various modules supported email, web browsing, (link: /terms/ftp text: FTP), and (link: /terms/gsm text: GSM)-based SMS messaging.
