Title: Finder

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/frame text: frame) containing (link: /terms/method text: methods) or (link: /terms/method text: objects) that enumerate data items found to match criteria specified via the (link: /terms/find-slip text: Find slip).

Not to be confused with the [Finder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finder_(software)), the default file manager found in the various iterations of Mac OS.
