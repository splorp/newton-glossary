Title: NewtDb

----

Date: 10 March 2026

----

Text:

A relational (link: /terms/database text: database) developed by DMP Systems. Designed for fast information retrieval, the user can define multiple databases and different layouts for displaying information. NewtDb supports (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), input from a barcode scanner, and can use (link: /terms/newton-connection-kit text: Newton Connection Kit) for importing and exporting data from Mac OS and PC database (link: /terms/application text: applications).
