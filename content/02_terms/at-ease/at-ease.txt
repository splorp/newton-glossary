Title: At Ease

----

Date: 24 April 2026

----

Text:

Software developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that provided a simplified alternative to the Mac OS Finder. Individual users were granted limited access to various Mac OS features, safeguarding both the (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) and individual user data.

(link: /terms/classroom-exchange text: Classroom Exchange) transferred data between user accounts on an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) device and individual user folders on a Mac OS computer running At Ease.

(link: /terms/at-ease-for-workgroups text: At Ease for Workgroups) allowed users to log into to a common server from any Mac OS computer running the At Ease software.
