Title: Accent Key

----

Date: 20 March 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/character-key text: character key) located on a keyboard for typing the ‘grave’ and ‘tilde’ diacritic (link: /terms/character text: characters). This key can be used in combination with other keys to type characters with accents.
