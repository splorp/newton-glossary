Title: ELFtoPKG

----

Date: 26 May 2026

----

Text:

A programming tool developed by (link: /sources/paul-guyot text: Paul Guyot) included with the (link: /terms/dcl text: DCL) (Desktop Connection Library) framework. ELFtoPKG generates a Newton (link: /terms/package text: package) from an (link: /terms/elf text: ELF) (Executable and Linkable Format) shared Library.

ELFtoPKG is a modern replacement for the (link: /terms/packer text: Packer) tool included with Apple’s (link: /terms/newton-c-tools text: Newton C++ Tools) development environment.

Files created using the ELFtoNTK tool are generally identified by the (link: /terms/ntkc text: .ntkc) filename extension.
