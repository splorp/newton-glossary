Title: Scroll Arrows

----

Date: 30 October 2001

----

Text:

A pair of icon-based (link: /terms/button text: buttons) used to move (or (link: /terms/scroll text: scroll)) an area of content displayed on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

On an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300), pressing a (link: /terms/scroll-keys text: scroll key) performs the same action as tapping a scroll arrow button.
