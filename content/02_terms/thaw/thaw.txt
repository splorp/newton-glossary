Title: Thaw

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

To restore the activity of an (link: /terms/application text: application) or (link: /terms/extension text: extension) package. Thawing a (link: /terms/package text: package) is similar to activating an (link: /terms/external-store text: external store) — such as a (link: /terms/pc-card text: PC card) — that a package is stored on.

To deactivate a package, you (link: /terms/freeze text: freeze) it.

Freezing and thawing of packages can be performed manually using a utility such as (link: /terms/options text: Options).
