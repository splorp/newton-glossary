Title: Inker

----

Date: 03 October 2014

----

Text:

A low-level task in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) which takes user [input](/terms/input) and draws the appropriate data on the [screen](/terms/screen). In effect, converting physical [pen](/terms/pen) strokes into [electronic ink](/terms/electronic-ink).
