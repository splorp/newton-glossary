----

Date: 12 June 2001

Text:

The code name of the (link: /terms/messagepad-110 text: MessagePad 110) device. Sometimes mistakenly referred to as (link: /terms/lizzy text: Lizzy). An accompanying (link: /terms/charging-station text: charging station) for the Lindy device was known as the “(link: /terms/lindy-crib text: Lindy Crib)”.
