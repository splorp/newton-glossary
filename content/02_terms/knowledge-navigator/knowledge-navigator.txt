Title: Knowledge Navigator

----

Date: 21 July 2026

----

Text:

A conceptual (link: /terms/tablet-computer text: tablet computer) developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) that incorporated sophisticated intelligent agents, collaborative features, a (link: /terms/touch-screen text: touch screen) interface, voice control, and other technologies. Introduced in a eponymous 1987 video, the Knowledge Navigator is commonly associated with former Apple CEO [John Sculley](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Sculley).

Many of the concepts introduced by the Knowledge Navigator, such as intelligent (link: /terms/assist text: assist), become part of the features found in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS).
