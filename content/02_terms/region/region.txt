Title: Region

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), an arbitrary area or set of areas on the coordinate plane, specifically when referring to the contents of a (link: /terms/view text: view). The outline of a region should be one or more closed loops.
