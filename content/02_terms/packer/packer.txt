Date: 26 May 2026

Text:

A (link: /terms/macintosh-programmers-workshop text: Macintosh Programmers Workshop) tool included with the (link: /terms/newton-c-tools text: Newton C++ Tools) development environment that combines information contained in multiple source files into a Newton (link: /terms/package text: package).

The (link: /terms/elftopkg text: ELFtoPKG) utility is a modern replacement for Packer.
