Title: Newton Slide Show

----

Date: 14 May 2026

----

Text:

A proof of concept (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /sources/lee-moon text: Lee Moon) that displays a series of images on a Newton device.

(image: /assets/images/newton-slide-show-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of Newton Slide Show displaying an image of Lee Moon width: 233)

By default, Newton Slide Show automatically advanced through the images. However, the amount of time each image is displayed and a transition effect could be specified.

Manually navigating back and forth through the images is accomplished by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/universal-scroll-arrows text: universal scroll arrows).

Features from Newton Slide Show were eventually integrated into (link: /terms/newton-movie-player text: Newton Movie Player), including the ability to add an audio track.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nmp text: NSS).
