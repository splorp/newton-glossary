Title: RISC

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

Reduced Instruction Set Computer

A processor designed to execute a rapid sequence of simple instructions rather than a large variety of complex instructions.

The (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000) and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices use a (link: /terms/strongarm text: StrongARM) RISC processor.
