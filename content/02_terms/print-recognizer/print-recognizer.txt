Title: Print Recognizer

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

The algorithmic component of the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system that identifies printed (link: /terms/character text: characters), as opposed to (link: /terms/cursive text: cursive) or handwritten text.

In prerelease versions of Newton OS 1.0, it is known as a (link: /terms/shape-interpreter text: Text Interpreter).

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/disconnected-recognizer text: Disconnected Recognizer) and (link: /terms/text-recognizer text: Text Recognizer).
