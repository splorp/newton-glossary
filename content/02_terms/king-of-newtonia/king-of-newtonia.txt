Title: King of Newtonia

----

Date: 21 November 2004

----

Text:

A nickname for the otherwise practical [Rich Lindsay](/sources/rich-lindsay), proprietor of the [This Old Newt](/terms/this-old-newt) website. The title of king was proclaimed by acclamation during the 2001 [Newtie Awards](/terms/newtie-award).
