Title: Silkscreen Button

----

Date: 30 October 2001

----

Text:

A colloquial term for one of the [hard buttons](/terms/hard-button) found in the [button bar](/terms/button-bar) below the [screen](/terms/screen) of early Newton devices, including the [OMP](/terms/omp), [MessagePad 100](/terms/messagepad-100), [MessagePad 110](/terms/messagepad-110), [MessagePad 120](/terms/messagepad-120), and [MessagePad 130](/terms/messagepad-130). So called because the [icon](/terms/icon) representing each [button](/terms/button) is silkscreened onto the surface of the device.

Also referred to as a [Silk Button](/terms/silk-button).
