Title: Find

----

Date: 01 February 2026

----

Text:

A system-wide feature built into (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) allows the user to search (link: /terms/application text: applications) for (link: /terms/text text: text), (link: /terms/dates text: dates), or other types of data.

(link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the Find (link: /terms/button text: button) located in the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) opens a (link: /terms/find-slip text: Find slip) where the user can specify what to look for.

(image: /assets/images/find-slip-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Find Slip width: 320)

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/search-engine text: Search Engine).
