Title: Shuffler

----

Date: 15 April 2026

----

Text:

An (link: /terms/application text: application) developed by (link: /terms/avanti text: Avanti Software) that allows the user to add, remove, edit, organize, and (link: /terms/route text: route) internet bookmarks. Bookmarked URLs could be opened in other applications such as web browsers or news readers.

Shuffler also included a feature called (link: /terms/shuffscope text: ShuffScope) mode, which provides a simple way to open or add a URL by (link: /terms/highlighting text: highlighting) the link and (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) an (link: /terms/icon text: icon) in a small (link: /terms/floating-container-view text: floating container view).

(image: /assets/images/shuffscope-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Shuffler ShuffScope palette width: 137)

Using the integrated application programming interface ((link: /terms/api text: API)), developers could utilize Shuffler to handle organization and storage of internet bookmarks for their own applications.
