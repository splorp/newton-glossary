Title: Close Box

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A small (link: /terms/button text: button) containing an “X” (link: /terms/character text: character), located in the lower right corner of a (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) or (link: /terms/slip text: slip). (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the button dismisses or closes the view.

On an (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300), pressing the (link: /terms/close-key text: Close key) performs the same action as tapping the close box button.

Compare with the (link: /terms/large-close-box text: large close box).
