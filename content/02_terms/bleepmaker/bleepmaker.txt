Title: BleepMaker

----

Date: 13 January 2025

----

Text:

Software developed by (link: /sources/makoto-kisanuki text: Makoto Kisanuki) for creating and editing sounds on a Newton device. BleepMaker utilizes a frequency modulation (FM) generator that synthesizes sounds by combining individual tones. A sound can be exported and used as an (link: /terms/alert-sound text: alert sound). Parameters for sounds can also be exported as hexadecimal data for viewing in the (link: /terms/Inspector text: Inspector).
