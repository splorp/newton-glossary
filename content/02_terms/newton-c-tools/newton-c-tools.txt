Title: Newton C++ Tools

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A complete (link: /terms/macintosh-programmers-workshop text: Macintosh Programmers Workshop) development environment that facilitates the creation and preparation of C++ code for inclusion in (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript) programs.

Erroneously referred to as a (link: /terms/newton-c-toolbox text: Newton C++ Toolbox) in the (link: /sources/lantern text: Lantern Data Link Layer Tools) reference.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/nct text: NCT).
