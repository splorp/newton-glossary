Title: Recognition Button

----

Date: 05 November 2017

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/picture-button text: picture button) containing an (link: /terms/icon text: icon) representing an “A” (link: /terms/character text: character).

When (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped), it displays an (link: /terms/recognizer-picker text: recognizer picker) allowing the user to select the method of (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) used to interpret what is being written or drawn.

Options include (link: /terms/text text: Text), (link: /terms/ink-word text: Ink Text), (link: /terms/shapes text: Shapes), and (link: /terms/sketches text: Sketches).

In (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works), the (link: /terms/quick-sketch text: Quick Sketch) slip also has a recognition button, but only the the Shapes and Sketches options are included.

(image: /assets/images/recognizer-picker-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of a Recognition Button with Picker width: 121)

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/recognizer-button text: Recognizer Button).
