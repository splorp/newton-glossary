Title: Spell Check Slip

----

Date: 22 June 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/slip text: slip) that lets the user identify and replace misspelled words in a (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) document are spelled correctly.

(image: /assets/images/spell-check-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Spell Check slip in Newton Works width: 261)

(link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the (link: /terms/tools-button text: Tools button) located in the (link: /terms/status-bar text: status bar) and selecting “Spell Check” in the (link: /terms/picker text: picker) displays the (link: /terms/spell-check text: Spell Check) slip.
