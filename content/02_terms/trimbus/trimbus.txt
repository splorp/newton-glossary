Title: TrimBus

----

Date: 04 July 2026

----

Text:

A superset of the (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) standard, developed by Pete Foley at Apple for the Newton platform.

All Newton devices have at least one PCMCIA (link: /terms/card-slot text: card slot) compatible with Apple’s proprietary TrimBus. This 32-bit bus is able to support additional storage, communication, networking, and other peripheral hardware.
