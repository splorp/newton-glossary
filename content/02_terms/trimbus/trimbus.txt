Title: TrimBus

----

Date: 04 July 2026

----

Text:

A proprietary superset of the (link: /terms/pcmcia text: PCMCIA) standard, developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) hardware engineer Pete Foley specifically for use on the Newton platform.

Whereas PCMCIA supports an 8-bit or 16-bit data bus by default, TrimBus implements a 32-bit data bus, allowing Newton devices to access larger amounts of memory on (link: /terms/storage card text: storage cards).
