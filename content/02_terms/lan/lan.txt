Local Area Network

A data communications network that covers a small or geographically limited area. A LAN typically facilitates the interconnection of computers and other devices within a single building or multiple adjacent buildings. (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet), (link: /terms/localtalk text: LocalTalk), and (link: /terms/wi-fi text: Wi-Fi) are examples of local area networks.
