Title: Power Switch

----

Date: 16 March 2026

----

Text:

A physical (link: /terms/button text: button) that turns a device on and off.

On (link: /terms/messagepad-130 text: MessagePad 130), (link: /terms/messagepad-2000 text: MessagePad 2000), and (link: /terms/messagepad-2100 text: MessagePad 2100) devices, the power switch also controls the (link: /terms/backlight text: backlight) in the (link: /terms/screen text: screen). Sliding and holding down the power switch for two seconds will turn on the backlight.

Rather than a sliding switch, the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) has a (link: /terms/power-key text: Power key) on the keyboard.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/backlighting-switch text: Backlighting Switch) or (link: /terms/Power Button text: Power Button).
