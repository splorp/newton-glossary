Title: BBEditor

----

Date: 25 January 2026

----

Text:

A system enhancement developed by (link: /sources/byte-size text: Byte-Size Software) consisting of a set of (link: /terms/button text: buttons) that provide quick access for editing, navigating, and (link: /terms/highlighting text: highlighting) and (link: /terms/select text: selecting) text, entering punctuation and user-definable (link: /terms/character text: characters), accessing the (link: /terms/clipboard text: clipboard), saving and retrieving (link: /terms/text text: text) clippings, as well as changing (link: /terms/font text: font) attributes.

When activated, BBEditor overlay a portion of the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar), not the (link: /terms/input-area text: input area).

(image: /assets/images/bbeditor-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the BBEditor overlaying the button bar on a MessagePad 2000 width: 322)
