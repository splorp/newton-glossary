Title: Restore Slip

----

Date: 20 April 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/container-view text: container view) providing an interface that allows the user to restore information from a (link: /terms/backup text: backup).

When a (link: /terms/storage-card text: storage card) is inserted into the (link: /terms/card-slot text: card slot), a (link: /terms/card-slip text: card slip) appears. If a (link: /terms/backup-file text: backup file) exists on the card, tapping the Restore (link: /terms/button text: button) in the card slip opens a restore slip. The user can then select which (link: /terms/application text: application) information and (link: /terms/package text: packages) to copy from the backup to the Newton device.
