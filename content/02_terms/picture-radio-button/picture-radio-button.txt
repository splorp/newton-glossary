Title: Picture Radio Button

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A Newton-specific (link: /terms/radio-button text: radio button) control where each button is represented by an (link: /terms/icon text: icon) or a (link: /terms/picture text: picture). Like a standard radio button, it displays its state — either on or off — and is part of a group in which the user can (link: /terms/select text: select) only one (link: /terms/button text: button) at a time. 

Typically, several picture radio buttons will appear next to each other in a (link: /terms/slip text: slip), with the selection indicated by a (link: /terms/thick-border text: thick border).
