----

Date: 09 March 2026

Text:

An alternate (and erroneously applied) name for (link: /terms/intelligent-newton-magazine text: Intelligent Newton Magazine).

NewtonWorld was also the title of special report focused on the Newton platform that was inserted into the December 1995 issue of [PC World](https://web.archive.org/web/19980214131454/https://www.pcworld.com/) magazine.
