Title: To Do Task

----

Date: 24 April 2026

----

Text:

A item in the (link: /terms/to-do-list text: To Do List).

Information regarding a (link: /terms/task text: task) appears in a (link: /terms/slip text: slip) and can include a title, a date when the task is to be completed, a (link: /terms/priority text: priority) level, the number of days until a reminder is displayed, and the option to have the task repeat after a specific period.
