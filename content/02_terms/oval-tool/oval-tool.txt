Title: Oval Tool

----

Date: 17 February 2026

----

Text:

A tool found in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) used to add elliptical (link: /terms/object text: objects) to a (link: /terms/drawing text: drawing) document.

(link: /terms/dragging text: Dragging) the oval tool on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen) using the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) will create an (link: /terms/oval text: oval). To create a (link: /terms/circle text: circle), hold down the (link: /terms/shift-key text: Shift key) while dragging the tool.

Tapping on the oval will cause a (link: /terms/selection-box text: selection box) to to appear around the object, allowing the user to resize the oval using the (link: /terms/handle text: handles).

Also referred to as the (link: /terms/circle-tool text: Circle Tool).
