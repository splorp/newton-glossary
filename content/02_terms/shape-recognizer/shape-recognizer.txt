Title: Shape Recognizer

----

Date: 28 January 2026

----

Text:

The algorithmic component of the (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) system that identifies both simple and complex geometric (link: /terms/object text: objects), cleaning up drawings to produce straight lines and smooth curves. The shape recognizer also identifies symmetry, if that property is present in an drawn object.

In prerelease versions of Newton OS 1.0, it is known as a (link: /terms/shape-interpreter text: Shape Interpreter).
