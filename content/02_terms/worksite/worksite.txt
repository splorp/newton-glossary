Title: Worksite

----

Date: 10 January 2018

----

Text:

An entry in the [Owner Info](/terms/owner-info) application where a Newton user establishes the address and other information related to a specific location. For example, a user can configure different worksites for home and office. A worksite can include information such as street address, telephone number, dialing prefix, time zone, printer settings, and email address.

Also referred to as an [Emporium](/terms/emporium) or [Locale](/terms/locale).
