----

Date: 13 February 2026

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), a (link: /terms/data-translator text: data translator) that converts a (link: /terms/binary-object text: binary object) containing a (link: /terms/flattened-frame text: flattened frame) into a (link: /terms/frame text: frame).

To perform the opposite translation, you would use a (link: /terms/flattener text: flattener) translator.
