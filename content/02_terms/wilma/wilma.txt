Title: WILMA

----

Date: 20 May 2024

----

Text:

Windows, iOS, Linux, Mac OS, and Android

An acronym coined by (link: /sources/matthias-melcher text: Matthias Melcher) that combines the initial letters of various computing platforms used in modern Newton software development. It is used in a chart explaining how various development tools are interconnected as documented in the (link: /terms/undo-overview text: Unified Newton Development Overview).

WILMA is also an acronym for the phrase “Will It Live (on my) Machine Also”.
