Title: Easter Egg

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A message, image, inside joke, or feature that has been intentional hidden in an (link: /terms/application text: application), (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system), or other media. Easter eggs are generally activated by a series of undocumented commands or actions.

Examples of (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) easter eggs include (link: /terms/area-51 text: Area 51) and (link: /terms/egg-freckles text: Egg Freckles).
