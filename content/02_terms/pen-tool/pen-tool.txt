Title: Pen Tool

----

Date: 23 June 2026

----

Text:

A tool found in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) used to specify attributes of (link: /terms/line text: lines) and edges of (link: /terms/object text: objects) in a (link: /terms/drawing text: drawing) document.

(link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the pen tool (link: /terms/button text: button) in the (link: /terms/toolbar text: toolbar) displays a (link: /terms/palette text: palette) with two sections. The “Pen Shade” section of the palette contains various shades of grey. The “Pen Size” section contain various widths of line.

Attributes can be applied to an existing (link: /terms/object text: object) by selecting it with the (link: /terms/pointer-tool text: pointer tool) and then tapping on the various pen shades and sizes in the palette.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/line-button text: Line Button) and (link: /terms/pen-button text: Pen Button).
