Title: Pen Tool

----

Date: 23 June 2026

----

Text:

A tool found in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) used to specify the visual attributes of (link: /terms/line text: lines) and edges of (link: /terms/object text: objects) in a (link: /terms/drawing text: drawing) document.

(image: /assets/images/pen-tool-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Pen Tool palette in Newton Works width: 200)
 
(link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the pen tool (link: /terms/button text: button) in the (link: /terms/toolbar text: toolbar) displays a (link: /terms/palette text: palette).

The (link: /terms/pen-shade text: Pen Shade) section of the palette contains various shades of grey.
The (link: /terms/pen-size text: Pen Size) section contain various widths of line.

Attributes can be applied to an existing (link: /terms/object text: object) by selecting it with the (link: /terms/pointer-tool text: pointer tool) and tapping on a pen shade or size in the palette.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/line-button text: Line Button) and (link: /terms/pen-button text: Pen Button).
