Title: Rounded Rectangle

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/rectangle text: rectangle) with rounded corners. The (link: /terms/shape text: shape) is defined by the rectangle itself, along with the diameter of the circles forming the corners (called the diameter of curvature).
