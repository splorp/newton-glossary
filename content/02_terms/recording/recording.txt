Title: Recording

----

Date: 28 September 2017

----

Text:

A type of (link: /terms/stationery text: stationery) in the (link: /terms/notepad text: Notepad) application that can be used to record and play sounds and voice messages.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/recorded-note text: Recorded Note), (link: /terms/recording-note text: Recording Note), and (link: /terms/sound-stationery text: Sound Stationery).

Recording is also a section found in the (link: /terms/prefs text: Prefs) application.

In the Recording (link: /terms/slip text: slip), the user can specify the audio source, as well as the quality and (link: /terms/volume text: volume) of the recording.
