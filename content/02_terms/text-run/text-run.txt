Title: Text Run

----

Date: 24 May 2001

Text:

A sequence of (link: /terms/character text: characters) displayed with the same (link: /terms/font-style text: font style) and (link: /terms/font-size text: font size) attributes. Text is represented in paragraph views as a series of text runs with corresponding (link: /terms/font-spec text: font spec) information.
