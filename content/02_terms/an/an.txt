Title: AN

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

After Newton

The period of time that has elapsed since the Newton was discontinued. Generally agreed to be any point after [February 27th, 1998](https://web.archive.org/web/20040528183253/http://www.apple.com/pr/library/1998/feb/27newton.html).

AN is also an abbreviation for the (link: /terms/action-names text: Action Names) software package.
