Title: Square

----

Date: 15 April 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/shape text: shape) defined by two points — its top-left and its bottom-right corners — or by four boundaries - its upper, left, bottom, and right sides.

A square is a (link: /terms/rectangle text: rectangle) whose sides are equal in length.
