Title: Scroll Keys

----

Date: 18 March 2026

----

Text:

A pair of (link: /terms/function-key text: function keys) located on the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) keyboard used to move (or (link: /terms/scroll text: scroll)) an area of content displayed on the (link: /terms/screen text: screen).

Pressing a scroll key performs the same action as tapping a (link: /terms/scroll-arrows text: scroll arrow) button.
