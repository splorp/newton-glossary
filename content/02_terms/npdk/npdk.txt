Text:

NewtPod Developer Kit

A collection of hardware items bundled together by (link: /sources/adriano-angelillis text: Adriano Angelillis) to facilitate experimenting with his (link: /terms/newtpod text: NewtPod) accessory. Included in the NPDK were the (link: /terms/newtpod-le text: NewtPod LE), (link: /terms/newtpod-ap text: NewtPod AP), an [iPod Remote](https://apple.fandom.com/wiki/IPod_Remote) plug, and an Apple [PlainTalk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PlainTalk) microphone.
