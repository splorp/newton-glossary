Title: Fill Tool

----

Date: 25 June 2026

----

Text:

A tool found in (link: /terms/newton-works text: Newton Works) used to specify a visual attribute to the interior of (link: /terms/object text: objects) in a (link: /terms/drawing text: drawing) document.

(image: /assets/images/fill-tool-2x.gif alt: Screenshot of the Fill Tool palette in Newton Works width: 228)
 
(link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the fill tool (link: /terms/button text: button) in the (link: /terms/toolbar text: toolbar) displays a (link: /terms/palette text: palette).

A fill attribute can be applied to an existing (link: /terms/object text: object) by selecting it with the (link: /terms/pointer-tool text: pointer tool) and tapping on a shade of grey or pattern in the palette. The interior of an object can also be set to transparent.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/fill-button text: Fill Button), (link: /terms/fill-pop-up-list text: Fill Pop-Up List), and (link: /terms/pattern-button text: Pattern Button).
