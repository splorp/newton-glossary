Title: Find Button

----

Date: 31 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/picture-button text: picture button) located in the (link: /terms/button-bar text: button bar) with an (link: /terms/icon text: icon) that looks like a magnifying glass. (link: /terms/tapping text: Tapping) the Find button opens a (link: /terms/find-slip text: Find slip), where the user can specify what to search for.
