Title: WLAN

----

Date: 08 November 2025

----

Text:

Wireless Local Area Network

A data communications network that provides connectivity to wireless devices within a limited area using a (link: /terms/wireless-access-point text: wireless access point). A WLAN is commonly deployed alongside (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet) in order that both wireless and wired devices can exchange data on the same network.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/wireless-lan text: Wireless LAN).
