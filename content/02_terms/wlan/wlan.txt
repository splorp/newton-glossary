Title: WLAN

----

Date: 08 November 2025

----

Text:

Wireless Local Area Network

A data communications network that provides connectivity to wireless devices within a limited area using a (link: /terms/wireless-access-point text: wireless access point). A WLAN is commonly deployed alongside (link: /terms/ethernet text: Ethernet) to support both wireless and wired devices on the same network.

Also referred to as (link: /terms/wireless-lan text: Wireless LAN).

Compare with a (link: /terms/wwan text: WWAN) (Wireless Wide Area Network) which covers a large geographical area, usually over a cellular network.
