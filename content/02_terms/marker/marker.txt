Title: Marker

----

Date: 18 September 2024

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/user-interface text: user interface) element that acts as an (link: /terms/indicator text: indicator) of magnitude, position, probability, degree, period of time, or some other amount within a range of possible values.

The diamond-shaped marker found on a (link: /terms/slider text: slider) control is adjustable, allowing the user to (link: /terms/drag text: drag) the marker to change the value.

The vertical marker associated with a (link: /terms/meeting text: meeting) in the (link: /terms/date-book text: Date Book) is also adjustable, allowing the user to change the time and duration of the meeting.

An adjustable marker can also be referred to as a (link: /terms/knob text: Knob).
