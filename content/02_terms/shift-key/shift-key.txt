Title: Shift Key

----

Date: 22 March 2026

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/modifier-key text: modifier key) used to change the (link: /terms/character text: character) produced when typing a specific key on a (link: /terms/keyboard text: keyboard).

When used with an alphabetic key, the Shift key will produce capital letters. When used with numeric or non-alphabetic keys, the Shift key will produce various punctuation marks and symbols.

Unlike the shift key on typewriter or computer, the (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) Shift key is not pressed concurrently with pressing a character key. The Shift key toggles on when (link: /terms/tapped text: tapped) and releases automatically when a character key is tapped.

This is similar to the functionality of the (link: /terms/caps-lock-key text: Caps Lock key), however the Caps Lock key has no effect on non-alphabetic keys.
