Title: Spring Issue

----

Date: 18 March 2021

----

Text:

The potential and almost inevitable failure of the tension spring in the hinge of an eMate (link: /terms/clamshell text: clamshell) lid. The constant opening and closing of the lid can cause the spring to unseat and puncture the ribbon cable connected to the (link: /terms/display text: display). Failure of the spring often results in a case of (link: /terms/floppy-lid text: floppy lid).
