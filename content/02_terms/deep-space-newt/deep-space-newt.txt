Title: Deep Space Newt

----

Date: 16 February 2017

----

Text:

A game for (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS) developed by (link: /sources/dejavu text: DejaVu Software) derived from the original text-based [Star Trek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Trek_(text_game)) game. Also referred to as (link: /terms/dsn text: DSN) and (link: /terms/dsnewt text: DSNewt), DejaVu ported the game to the iOS platform in 2008.
