Title: Pen Holder

----

Date: 10 December 2023

----

Text:

A slot or well built into a Newton device to store or hold the (link: /terms/pen text: pen) when not in use. Along with a pair of wells on either side of the keyboard, the (link: /terms/emate-300 text: eMate 300) also has a recessed (link: /terms/pen-tray text: pen tray) to store the pen when the lid is closed.

The (link: /terms/charging-station text: charging station) accessory manufactured by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) and some third-party stands, such as (link: /terms/the-executive text: The Executive), also included a pen holder.
