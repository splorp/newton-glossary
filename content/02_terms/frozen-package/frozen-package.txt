Title: Frozen Package

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A (link: /terms/package text: package) that has been manually deactivated by (link: /terms/freezing text: freezing).

When a package is frozen, the (link: /terms/icon text: icon) changes to a (link: /terms/snowflake text: snowflake) and can be activated by (link: /terms/tapping text: tapping) the (link: /terms/icon text: icon).

Freezing and thawing of packages can be performed using a utility such as (link: /terms/options text: Options).

In comparison, a (link: /terms/suppressed-package text: suppressed package) is a package that has been temporarily deactivated by the operating system.

Also referred to as a (link: /terms/deactivated-package text: deactivated package) or (link: /terms/inactive-package text: inactive package).
