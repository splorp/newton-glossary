Title: Long Term Storage

----

Date:

----

Text:

An extended period of disuse of a Newton device or other piece of equipment. Part of the preparation process prior to long term storage is to remove the main [battery](/terms/battery) and the [backup battery](/terms/backup-battery), if applicable. This helps prevent damage to the Newton device due to battery leakage.

Also referred to as [LTS](/terms/lts).
