Title: Display Area

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

The portion of a Newton device [screen](/terms/screen) on which information is displayed. As opposed to the [non-display area](/terms/non-display-area) between the display area and edge of the case or [bezel](/terms/bezel) surrounding the screen.
