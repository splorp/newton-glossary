Title: Display Area

----

Date: 18 December 2001

----

Text:

The portion of a Newton device (link: /terms/screen text: screen) on which information is displayed. As opposed to the (link: /terms/non-display-area text: non-display area) between the display area and edge of the case or (link: /terms/bezel text: bezel) surrounding the screen.
