Title: Developer Technical Services

----

Date: 24 August 2016

----

Text:

A group within [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) which provided technical support and programming tools for third-party software and hardware developers. Commonly known as (link: /terms/dts text: DTS). The current group is now called [Apple Developer Support](https://developer.apple.com/support/).

The technical services group that supported Newton developers was known as (link: /terms/pie-dts text: PIE DTS).
