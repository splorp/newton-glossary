Title: Tag

----

Date: 06 October 2002

----

Text:

In (link: /terms/newtonscript text: NewtonScript), an optional (link: /terms/symbol text: symbol) used to mark one or more (link: /terms/soup text: soup) entries. The (link: /terms/operating-system text: operating system) treats a missing tag as a (link: /terms/nil text: nil) value.

Tag is also a reference to the (link: /terms/icon text: icon) used to identify the (link: /terms/tag-button text: Tag Button).
