Title: Caret

----

Date:

----

Text:

A [cursor](/terms/cursor) symbol (^) that indicates where the Newton will insert text written, printed, or typed by the user. When [tapped](/terms/tapped), a [caret picker](/terms/caret-picker) is displayed allowing the user to insert punctuation marks, spaces, paragraph breaks, or other special characters. The caret is only used in [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os) 2.0 and later. Earlier versions of Newton OS use a [blinking line](/terms/blinking-line) cursor to indicate where new text will be inserted.

A caret shaped [gesture](/terms/gesture) can be used to indicate where a new [insertion point](/terms/insertion-point) should be placed when editing text.
