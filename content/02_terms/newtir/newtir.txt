Title: NewtIR

----

Date:

----

Text:

An [infrared](/terms/infrared) communications [protocol](/terms/protocol) based on the [ASK](/terms/ask) standard and used in older Newton devices. This protocol includes extensions developed by [Apple](https://www.apple.com/) which make it substantially faster.
