Title: ParaGraph

----

Date: 07 September 2017

----

Text:

The company responsible for the first generation of (link: /terms/handwriting-recognition text: handwriting recognition) that was included in (link: /terms/newton-os text: Newton OS). They later developed the (link: /terms/cursive-recognition text: cursive recognition) system for Newton OS 2.0, sometimes referred to as the (link: /terms/paragraph-recognizer text: Paragraph Recognizer). Commercially known as (link: /terms/calligrapher text: CalliGrapher), the software was also licensed to Microsoft for use in some of their tablet computers.
