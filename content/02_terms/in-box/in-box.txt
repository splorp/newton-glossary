Title: In Box

----

Date: 24 May 2001

----

Text:

A list in the [InOut Box](/terms/inout-box) application where received items can be viewed, filed, [routed](/terms/routing), and otherwise managed. The In Box and [Out Box](/terms/out-box) are separate [applications](/terms/application) in earlier versions of [Newton OS](/terms/newton-os).
